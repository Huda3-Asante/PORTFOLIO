Quality Education for Every Child

At Tema New Church School, we believe in providing an inclusive, nurturing, and high-quality educational experience for every child. Our dedicated staff fosters a love for learning and personal growth.

Achievements

  • BECE Results: Consistently achieving a 95% pass rate in the BECE, with many students qualifying for prestigious senior high schools.
  • Spelling Bee: Our reading club members secured 5th position in the Ghana National Spelling Bee Championship, competing against 57 contestants from multiple regions.

Empowering Young Minds

Our curriculum is designed to inspire curiosity and encourage critical thinking. From foundational subjects like Mathematics and Science to Languages and Arts, we aim to equip students with the skills needed for future success.

  • Inclusive environment promoting diversity and respect.
  • Focus on holistic development: academic, physical, and emotional.
  • Engagement in extracurricular activities to enhance student life.

Our experienced teachers implement innovative teaching methods and provide personalized support to help each student thrive. We are committed to fostering a lifelong love for learning in our students.

At Tema New Church School, we strive to create a strong sense of community among students, parents, and staff. Through collaboration, we ensure that every child's educational journey is meaningful and impactful. Join us in shaping the leaders of tomorrow!

Extracurricular Activities

At Tema New Church School, we believe that learning extends beyond the classroom. Our extracurricular activities provide students with the opportunity to explore their interests, develop new skills, and build lasting friendships.

  • Debate Club

    Sharpen your communication and critical thinking skills by joining our Debate Club, where students engage in thoughtful discussions on various topics.

  • Sports Club

    Whether you're into football, basketball, or athletics, our Sports Club is the perfect place to stay active, foster teamwork, and participate in competitive games.

  • Reading Club

    For book lovers and aspiring writers, our Reading Club offers a space to explore literature, discuss favorite books, and improve reading comprehension.

  • Cadet Troupe

    Join the Cadet Troupe to develop leadership, discipline, and teamwork skills through structured drills and community service activities.

  • Cultural Troupe

    Celebrate diversity and heritage by being part of the Cultural Troupe, where students showcase traditional dances, music, and performances from various cultures.
