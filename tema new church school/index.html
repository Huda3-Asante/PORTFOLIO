Frequently Asked Questions

Explore answers to common inquiries about Tema New Church School

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements include submission of previous school records, completion of an admission form, and an entrance examination for certain grades.

What extracurricular activities do you offer?

We offer a variety of extracurricular activities such as sports, music, drama, art, and clubs catering to diverse interests.

How can I schedule a school tour?

To schedule a school tour, please contact our admissions office. Tours are available on weekdays from 9 AM to 3 PM.

Is financial aid available?

Yes, we offer financial aid and scholarships based on merit and need. Please inquire with our financial aid office for more information.

What are the school hours?

School hours are from 8 AM to 3 PM, Monday through Friday. After-school programs are available until 5 PM.

How can I get in touch with the school administration?

You can contact the school administration by phone at +233 234567890 or email newchurchschooltema@yahoo.com. Our office hours are Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM.
