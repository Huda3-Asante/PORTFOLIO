Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

The timeline varies depending on the project scope and complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 3-6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's your pricing structure?

I offer both fixed-price projects and hourly rates, depending on the project requirements. Fixed prices are great for well-defined projects, while hourly rates work better for ongoing work or projects with evolving requirements.

Do you work with remote clients?

Yes! I work with clients from all over the world. I'm experienced in remote collaboration and use various tools to ensure smooth communication and project management.

What technologies do you work with?

I work with a wide range of technologies including FastAPI, MongoDB, SQL and Python. I choose the best technology stack for each project based on its specific requirements.
