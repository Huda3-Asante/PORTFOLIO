Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions I get from clients. Click a question to reveal the answer.

How long does a typical project take?

The timeline varies depending on the project scope and complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 3-6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's your pricing structure?

I offer both fixed-price projects and hourly rates, depending on the project requirements. Fixed prices are great for well-defined projects, while hourly rates work better for ongoing work or projects with evolving requirements.

Do you work with remote clients?

Yes! I work with clients from all over the world. I'm experienced in remote collaboration and use various tools to ensure smooth communication and project management.

What technologies do you work with?

I work with a wide range of technologies including React, Vue, Node.js and Python. I choose the best technology stack for each project based on its specific requirements.

Can you help update or redesign an existing website?

Absolutely! Whether you need small tweaks, a fresh redesign, or a complete overhaul, I can work with your existing setup to bring it up to modern standards.

Do you offer ongoing support after a project is completed?

Yes, I provide maintenance and support packages to ensure your website or application stays updated, secure, and running smoothly.

Of course! I offer a free initial consultation where we can discuss your ideas, goals, and project requirements before moving forward.
